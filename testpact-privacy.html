TestPact Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 24, 2026

TestPact ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Done Deal Entertainment LLC, Chicago, IL. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the TestPact app.

Information We Collect

We collect your Google account email address when you sign in. We also collect app information you provide during onboarding, testing feedback you submit, screenshots you upload, and device push notification tokens for sending assignment notifications.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to match you with app testing assignments, notify you of new assignments and status updates, track credits earned and testers received, and improve the TestPact platform.

Data Sharing

Your Gmail address is shared with app developers whose apps you are assigned to test, so they can add you to their Google Play closed testing group. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services, including Firestore and Firebase Storage. Screenshots you upload are stored in Firebase Storage.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at issac@donedealstudios.com.

Children's Privacy

TestPact is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
